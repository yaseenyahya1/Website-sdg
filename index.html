About the Event

The Climate Action Hackathon 2025 brings together students, developers, and innovators to create digital solutions for climate action. The main goal is to reduce urban heat and improve flood resilience through creative prototypes and apps.

  • Workshops and mentoring sessions
  • Access to climate and geospatial datasets
  • Team-based collaboration and prizes

Connection to SDG 13 – Climate Action

This event supports SDG 13 by promoting projects that help reduce the effects of climate change. Participants can work on flood warning systems, heat maps, or sustainable infrastructure ideas.

Tracks / Challenges

  • Heat Mitigation – Ideas to cool urban areas using technology.
  • Flood Early Warning – Tools to alert communities before floods.

  • Community Apps – Platforms for public awareness and participation.
